77 Million Paintings

Inspiration: This project has been knocking around in my brain for the better part of a decade. The software, originally conceived by Brian Eno, procedurally generates art and music from a large array of source material. It is capable of generating 77 million different permutations of the included art, which when you do the math, predicts that you’ll never see the same image twice… even if you play it constantly for like a million years!

Full “build” album can be found here.
